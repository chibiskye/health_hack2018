Generalized

Breakfast - well-balanced and should be able to last you through most of the day
15-25% cal
Lunch - is the meal people are most likely to eat out, make sure to look at calories,
300-600 calories
Dinner - should always contain protein (ex. most meats, fish and eggs)
~600 calories
Snacks - Many people tend to overeat on snacks.
Fruit: no more than 3 servings
Nuts/Seeds: 1/4 quarter of nuts or seeds
~150-200 calories

Daily Intake for College Students (Males)

Males require an average of 2600 calories.
These values can vary +/- 200 calories depending on how active you are!
More if you are more active and vice versa

Daily amount of water: 3.7L (Always drink whenever you feel dehydrated or tired!)
Grains: 4-8 servings
Protein: 6.5 servings
Dairy: 3 servings
Fruits & Veggies: 5 servings

Daily Intake for College Students (Females)

Females require an average of 2000 calories.
These values can vary +/- 200 calories depending on how active you are!
More if you are more active and vice versa

Daily amount of water: 2.7L (Always drink whenever you feel dehydrated or tired!)
Grains: 3-5 servings
Protein: 5.5 servings
Dairy: 3 servings
Fruits & Veggies: 4.5 servings

Serving Sizes

Grains:
1 serving size =
.5 cup brown rice
.5 cup oatmeal
1 cup wheat cereal flakes
1 slice wheat bread

Protein: 5.5 servings
1 small steak = 3-4 servings
1 chicken breast = 3 servings
1 oz of nuts = 2 servings
3 eggs = 3 servings

Dairy: 3 servings
1 serving size =
1 cup milk
1 cup yogurt
2 cups cottage cheese
2 slices processed cheese
1.5 cups ice cream

Fruits & Veggies: 4.5 servings
Vegetables:
1 serving size =
3 5" crowns of broccoli
2 medium carrots
2 cups cooked spinach
1 large tomato
1 cup cooked beans
Fruits:
1 serving size =
1 apple/banana/orange
16 grapes
.5 grapefruit
4 oz. applesauce
