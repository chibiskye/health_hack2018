Why Should "We" College Students Eat Healthy

We, the people the college students, spend most of our time running all over our college campuses, following busy schedules crammed to the brim with science, mathematics, history, arts-- and let's not forget good old English course (don't be tricked by the fancy wording in "literature"). It's no surprise that we barely even have time to worry about our well-being. I mean, come on, there's two midterms tomorrow and you haven't even started studying for either of them yet, no thanks to the research paper you had to work on that was due yesterday and the huge project whose deadline is in 2 days. AGHHHHHHH!!!

Okay, okay, we get the point, we're busy-- but we shouldn't be so busy that we don't have time to eat a proper meal. In fact, not eating a proper meal can harm you more than benefit you, and not just any meal-- a health, nutritious meal. Don't have time to eat a proper meal? Taking a minute to grab a healthy snack or two won't jeopardize your grade. On the contrast, it might be just the thing you need to finally figure out the answer to that last question you were stuck on for over an hour.

Long story short, eat healthy and your grades will be healthy too ;)
